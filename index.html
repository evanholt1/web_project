ABOUT US

More than 20 years ago, Armand Moreno opened the first Silverline Gym™ branch.

It was located in Kansas,Texas. It offered basic fitness machines and training.
But nevertheless, Armand's Gym offered the chance for fitness enthusiasts
the family and enviroment they needed to grow and achieve.

The breakthrough came when the 4th branch was opened.
It was larger than any branch before, and offered accomodations and facilities
the likes the area had never seen. This was the beginning of Silveline Gym's success story.

The grand opening of the 13th Silverline Gym™ branch in Dubai
signalled the opening of a new chapter in the story of Silverline Gym.
It was in it's opening period one of the most popular gyms in the area,
as it offered the best experience for a growth enviroment.
The Dubai branch was also the first Silverline Gym™ branch to actually
hold bodybuilding competitions.

The Jordan branch of Silverline Gym™ is one of the Biggest in the Middle East,
boasting a high count of high quality training machines of all kinds,
to fit the fitness needs of everyone.
The branch has also recieved the direct attention of Armand, and thus more resources
have been spent on making the Jordan branch of Silverline Gym™ an extremely advanced
facility with high-quality grade training machines, to go along with the plethora
of activities that can be done within the branch.
Additionally, the branch has hired some of the most prominent & capable trainers
in the country, and thus offering one of the best training experiences that can be had in the region.

TRAINING

Why Take Personal Training?

People take Personal Training for many different reasons but generally they have a goal
or a result they want to achieve. Sometimes it is appearance, health or even performance.
It could be weight loss, toning, strengthening, body building or simply dropping a dress
size for that special event.
If you have a goal and are either struggling to achieve this yourself or want to achieve this
in a timescale that is as fast as possible then Personal Training is for you!

Motivation!

We believe that as a company we offer the best value Personal Training there is!
Our trainers are experts at helping you get the most out of every workout
– no cutting corners,just full balanced effort.
They are trained to deal with all sorts of injuries
and will give you their devoted attention day-in and day-out.
Frankly speaking, if you take personal training,
there is no way out before we get you to your goal! Be careful ;)

Safety

We are not always aware of how we do something or the effect
an exercise or a particular technique may have on our bodies.
While maximizing your benefit through hard work,
the trainer ensures you perform exercises
that are appropriate for your ability and current fitness level,
this reduces the likelihood of injury keeping
your training at the gym and not spending time on therapy.

Variety

We all get bored doing the same thing.
Trainers have an entire database of training methods
and specific exercises they can call on
to keep each and every session fresh and varied.
Many of these exercises you probably can’t do safely
or as effectively if you were to do them alone.
This in turn keeps you working in manner to maximize your results..
