ABOUT US

More than 20 years ago, Armand Moreno opened the first Silverline Gym branch.

It was located in Kansas,Texas. It offered basic fitness machines and training. But nevertheless, Armand's Gym offered the chance for fitness enthusiasts the family and enviroment they needed to grow and achieve.

The breakthrough came when the 4th branch was opened. It was larger than any branch before, and offered accomodations and facilities the likes the area had never seen. This was the beginning of Silveline Gym's success story.





The grand opening of the 13th Silverline Gym branch in Dubai signalled the opening of a new chapter in the story of Silverline Gym.It was in it's opening period one of the most popular gyms in the area, as it offered the best experience for a growth enviroment.

The Dubai branch was also the first Silverline Gym branch to actually hold bodybuilding competitions.








The Jordan branch of Silverline Gym is one of the Biggest in the Middle East, boasting a high count of high quality training machines of all kinds, to fit the fitness needs of everyone.

The branch has also recieved the direct attention of Armand, and thus more resources have been spent on making the Jordan branch of Silverline Gym an extremely advanced facility with high-quality grade training machines, to go along with the plethora of activities that can be done within the branch.

Additionally, the branch has hired some of the most prominent & capable trainers in the country, and thus offering one of the best training experiences that can be had in the region.
